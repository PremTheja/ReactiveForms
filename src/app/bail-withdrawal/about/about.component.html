About this form

You can use this online form to withdraw an existing bail application. You will need to upload your completed and signed withdrawal application form at the end of this process outlining the reason why you are seeking to withdraw the application.

Your bail application will not immediately be withdrawn when you file this form. Once your application for withdrawal has been considered, the Registry will email you to confirm either that the application has been withdrawn, or whether you are still required to attend court.

Things you need to know to file this form

  • You will be asked some basic questions about the form.
  • You will then be prompted to upload a completed and signed PDF version of the form.
  • The information on the uploaded form MUST match the information entered on the online form.
  • Visit the forms page to download the required form: Visit the forms page
  • The form will be subject to a Registry review and could be subject to rejection if not completed correctly.
  • You will receive an email to confirm that the form has been successfully filed.

Filing fees

This application does not have a filing fee.

Upload restrictions

  • Each file must not exceed 5MB in size, in PDF format only.
  • Files must not be password-protected or restricted.
  • File names must be under 100 characters and may only include A–Z, 0–9, hyphens (-), underscores (_), and full stops (.).
